About Pathagar | Lens

Connecting researchers with trusted scholarly resources

Our Mission

Pathagar Lens was created to simplify academic discovery. Our goal is to bring together trusted scholarly journals, open access repositories and indexed research databases into one unified search platform.

What We Offer

The platform allows researchers, students and institutions to explore peer-reviewed journals across multiple disciplines. We focus on reliable metadata, structured indexing and a distraction-free research interface.

Who It Is For

Pathagar Lens is built for serious academic work. Whether you are a university student, faculty member, independent researcher or institutional librarian, the platform is designed to support structured research workflows.
